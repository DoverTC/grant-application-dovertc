Dover Town Council Grant Application

Section A: Date of Application

Date this application was written and signed.

Section B: Applicant Details

Enter the name, address, and organisation applying for this funding.

Section C: Charity Number

Enter your charity number if applicable. Company number is not required.

Section D: Project Description

Tell us about the project/event/activity which this funding will make possible (up to 500 words).

Section E: List what you will spend the funding on.

List in detail what you will spend the money on. Please put cost against each item (up to 500 words). Give a total (up to £500) for how much funding you are applying for.

Section F: Event Details

Enter the date(s) and location of your event/project/activity including postcode.

Section G: Community Benefit

How will your event/project/activity benefit people who live or work in the THE TOWN OF DOVER (see map in guidance)

Section H: Beneficiaries

How many residents of Dover will benefit from this grant funding and how will you count them?

Section I: Contact Information

Name, Tel no & email address of the person who is completing this application. You will be contacted if there are any questions and notified whether the application has been successful.

Section J: Bank Details

Details of the bank account into which the grant funding will be paid - name on account, sort code & account No.

Section K: Supporting Signatures

Please name two Dover Town Councillors who support this application. Signatures will be collected separately.

Section L: Declaration

Please confirm the following before submission: I declare that,

  • The information in this application is true in all sections of this form.
  • I am authorised to apply for this grant funding on behalf of the organisation named on this form.
  • Funding is not being provided from any other source (please see guidance on and including income, government funding, or other grants) for the items listed in Section E.
  • I will ensure Dover Town Council’s contribution is publicised by the organisation (e.g., via social media, printed materials, radio/TV, or word-of-mouth).
  • I understand that if the funding is not used on the agreed items/event/project, or receipts are not provided, or if the application is found to be fraudulent, then I and the trustees/directors/committee members will be liable to repay the funding to Dover Town Council on demand.
